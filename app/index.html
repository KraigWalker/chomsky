I Am Noam.

Noam Chomsky is a US political theorist and activist, and insitute professor of linguistics at the Massachisetts Institute of Technology (MIT). Besides his work in linguistics, Chomsky is internationally recognized as on of the most critically engaged public intellectuals alive.

Chomsky continues to be an unapologetic critic of both American foreign policy and it's ambitions for geopolitical hegemony and the neoliberal turn of global capitalism, which he identifies in terms of class warfare waged from above against the neds and interests of the great majority.

Chomsky is also an incisive critic of the ideological role of the mainstream corportate mass media, which, he maintains, "manufactures consent" toward the desirability of capitalism and the political powers supportive of it.
